Individual Pathway

Start with the person, then connect the business case by case.

These existing builders create personal or public context files. They can stand alone for an individual, or connect to a business, artist practice, club, grant application or noticeboard publisher when that is the right fit.

Open existing builder

profile.md

Creates a public-facing personal profile: who someone is, where they belong, what they can offer, and what projects or community roles they want to be known for.

Use when a person needs a public doorway before linking them to a business, artist practice, committee role or grant applicant.

Open existing builder

aura.md

Creates deeper AI context for the person: intentions, values, tone, risks, preferences, planning context and reusable prompts.

Use when the grant work needs personal context, creative direction, decision support or a clearer voice behind the application.

Open existing builder

noticeboard.md

Creates a public notice file: what is safe to publish, who approves updates, what notices are useful, and what should never be public by default.

Use when a person, business, artist, group or service wants public updates connected to the Straddie noticeboard flow.
