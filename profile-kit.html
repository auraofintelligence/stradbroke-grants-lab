Profile Kit

Markdown packs for faster, cleaner grant drafting.

These are small files an AI agent can read before drafting. Think of them as labelled lunchboxes for facts: business profile, project story, noticeboard updates, readiness checks and milestone reports.

Applicant facts

business-profile.md

Who you are, ABN, structure, contact, insurance, past work and permission boundaries.

Project brain

aura.md

The deeper intention, values, risks, language style and what the public page should not claim.

Community updates

noticeboard.md

Short public updates, calls for partners, workshop notes and status changes.

Grant readiness

checklist.md

Eligibility, evidence, budget, timeline, quotes, permissions and reporting duties.

Accountability

milestone-report.md

Progress, spend, changes, evidence, next steps and acquittal notes.

Service Flow

How Luke can charge less time without doing less thinking

  1. Research once: keep the grant sources and local matches current.
  2. Profile once: build a reusable applicant and project pack.
  3. Customise fast: adapt the same truth into the grant's exact questions.
  4. Check hard: eligibility, claims, budget, quotes, permissions and reporting.
