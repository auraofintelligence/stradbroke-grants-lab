Workflow

From rough idea to funded and acquitted.

  1. Scope: applicant, project, place, beneficiaries and deadline.
  2. Match: choose grant level and test eligibility.
  3. Evidence: collect public benefit, quotes, permissions, budget and risks.
  4. Draft: write in the funder's language without over-claiming.
  5. Review: check rules, attachments, conflicts and reporting duties.
  6. Submit: record portal, date, version and contact person.
  7. Milestones: keep proof as work happens, not at the panic end.
